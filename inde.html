N8tive Awareness

N8tive Awareness is here to talk to and give advice to Native Americans whom feel like they are left out of society.

Our Goals

  1. To give the American Indian People hope.
  2. To provide people with a good understanding of the Native American culture.
  3. To make sure no one is feeling left out or behind.
